Guacamole page!

Description

A tried and true guacamole recipe that's easy to make, uses fresh ingredients and is loaded with flavor. It's the best guacamole dip hands down.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados
  • Small onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Cilantro
  • Jalapeno
  • Garlic
  • Lime
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. Slice vegetables
  2. Mix together in a bowl
  3. Add lime juice and salt
  4. Serve with tortilla chips
